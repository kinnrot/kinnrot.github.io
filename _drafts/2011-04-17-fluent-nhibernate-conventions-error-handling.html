--- layout: post title: fluent nhibernate conventions error handling date: '2011-04-17T08:28:00.001-07:00' author: Chen Kinnrot tags: modified_time: '2011-04-23T11:38:33.075-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8908829452516573489.post-1182144866341392475 --- Tip for the fluent nhibernate users:

For mapping conventions
If your code inside the Apply method throw exceptions (meaning you do more than put comments inside)
allways wrap it with try catch block, and write that the exception was throwned there for future maintainablity of your code.

It took me 5 minutes to understand where and why I get exception when I added a new entity mapping that didn't follow all other entities conventions.
